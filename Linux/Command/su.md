## su
Switching between users
```
tryhackme@linux2:~$ su user2
Password:
user2@linux2:/home/tryhackme$
```

### su -l
switch user and change dir to **user's home directory**
```
tryhackme@linux2:~$ su -l user2
Password:
user2@linux2:~$ pwd
user2@:/home/user2$
```
